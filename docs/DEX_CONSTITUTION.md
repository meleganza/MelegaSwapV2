---

## SECTION I — MISSION

Melega DEX is **not** a speculative exchange.

Melega DEX is **trusted economic infrastructure**.

Its purpose is to enable **AI-native and human-readable economic operations** through transparent, machine-readable infrastructure.

### Constitutional priorities

| Priority | Rule |
|----------|------|
| **Truth** | Truth has priority over hype. |
| **Infrastructure** | Infrastructure has priority over speculation. |
| **Transparency** | Transparency has priority over marketing. |

Melega DEX exists to **operate**, **earn**, **discover**, and **build** within a coherent economic civilization — not to manufacture short-term narrative cycles or fundraising events.

---

## SECTION II — CORE PRINCIPLES

Six principles define permanent responsibility across the ecosystem.

### Operate

**Responsibility:** Execute economic actions with integrity — swaps, liquidity, staking, and farm participation through validated infrastructure.

Operate means the DEX provides **reliable execution surfaces** where human intent becomes on-chain or dry-run economic action. Operate is not speculation; it is **infrastructure-backed execution**.

### Earn

**Responsibility:** Surface yield, staking, and reward opportunities with transparent parameters.

Earn covers Farms, Pools, and reward infrastructure. Earn must present **operational truth** — APR, TVL, emissions, and lock terms — not marketing promises.

### Discover

**Responsibility:** Help users and agents find projects, signals, and ranked opportunities.

Discover is served by Trending, Projects, and Radar. Discover **ranks and observes**; it does not launch or fundraise.

### Build

**Responsibility:** Create and import economic infrastructure — tokens, pools, farms, manifests, and extensions.

Build is the domain of Build Studio. Build produces **machine-readable infrastructure**, not speculative launches.

### Own

**Responsibility:** Represent identity, membership, and civilization rights through collectibles and future digital identities.

Own grants **civilization access** and identity privileges — not speculative NFT trading as primary purpose.

### AI

**Responsibility:** Every surface must be human-readable and machine-readable; AI assists validation, discovery, and infrastructure — never replaces constitutional truth.

AI is a **first-class architectural layer**, not a cosmetic overlay. Workflows produce structured outputs where appropriate.

## SECTION V — AI MODEL

### Philosophy

| Layer | Priority |
|-------|----------|
| **Human-first UX** | Users understand what they are doing in plain language. |
| **Machine-first architecture** | Systems emit structured, parseable, auditable outputs. |

### Requirements

Every constitutional page must be:

1. **Human readable** — clear labels, operational descriptions, no hype-first copy.
2. **Machine readable** — manifests, metadata, structured tables, and AI-classified fields where appropriate.

Every workflow that creates or modifies infrastructure must produce **structured machine-readable outputs** where appropriate (e.g. AI Manifest, validation reports, eligibility flags).

AI assists:

- Infrastructure validation (Build Studio)
- Operational discovery (Radar)
- Ranking signals (Trending)
- Advisory workflows (Build Studio AI Advisor)

AI must **not**:

- Replace constitutional truth with generated hype
- Bypass dry-run or certification gates (KERL)
- Imply execution where only dry-run is permitted
